Process

How ReviewMe works

Three simple steps between you and the honest feedback that actually moves your craft forward.

01
Submit your work
Upload images, paste a link, or share a text excerpt. Tell reviewers what you want feedback on — no vague "just general thoughts."
02
Strangers review anonymously
Real creatives from the community review your work with no identity attached. No followers, no social pressure — just honest craft critique.
03
Build credibility by reviewing
The more quality reviews you leave, the higher your credibility score. Better reviewers get more visibility. Honesty is rewarded here.
Integrity System

Why reviewers earn trust

Anonymous doesn't mean unaccountable. Our credibility system separates thoughtful critics from drive-by comments.

🎖️
Credibility Score
Every reviewer earns a score based on helpfulness votes. High-cred reviewers are flagged so you know whose opinion to weigh most.
🗳️
Helpful / Not Helpful
Submitters vote on every review they receive. Consistently unhelpful reviewers lose standing. The community self-moderates over time.
🔒
Full Anonymity
Reviewer identities are never revealed to submitters. This is structurally enforced — not just a policy. You can't game it socially.
⚖️
Critique Guidelines
Reviews must be specific, constructive, and respect the work's intent. Vague negativity gets flagged. This is brutal honesty, not cruelty.
